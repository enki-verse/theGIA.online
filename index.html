WHO WE ARE

We are becoming
gods — playfully.

The Great I Am is a magical, sensual art camp. This is our honest starting point.

We work with the generative current at the root of existence — the boundless flowing forth that animates all life. We treat this force as magical and our inheritance. We do not turn away from it. We meet it with clear attention and creative intent.

We bring the childlike freedom to step into our creative selves and our full power. We are becoming individuals who can change what is real around us, playfully, with the boundary-making powers of an adult. The body is the portal. Embodiment is our method of manifestation.

OUR FOUNDATION

Our unique values

These principles guide how we gather, create, and transform together.

The Body as Portal

Movement, costume, dance, portraiture and performance are how we channel. Embodiment is our method of manifestation. When we dress, move, paint and are painted, we tune ourselves and make things real.

The Erotic Charge

We recognise the erotic as the life-force at the base of existence and the universe. We work with it consciously and creatively as our birthright.

Childlike Freedom, Adult Power

We bring the childlike freedom to step into our creative selves — into our full power and magic. We change what is real around us, playfully, with the boundary-making powers of an adult.

Flow State

We live in the now and in the flow state. We use improv and intuitive movement. We allow the moment to be there rather than imposing on it. The present is always generative.

Comedy & Divinity

We embrace comedy as essential — a way of not taking ourselves too seriously — while truly believing we are becoming gods. These two things live together in us without contradiction.

The Art of Becoming

We make costumes. We tune ourselves to become magic and to channel. We make up stories and become characters. Becoming is real.

Beauty as Environment

Beauty matters. The paintings on the walls, the costumes, the quality of what we make — this is not decoration. It is the environment in which transformation becomes possible.

No Spectators

We collapse the distance between performer and audience. There are no spectators in GIA. If you are in the room, you are in it.

THE GIA ESSENCE

What makes GIA, GIA

The combination is what makes us distinct. We are magical and we mean it. We honour the sensual and vital currents that move through us and we name them. We are comedic and we use it as a portal. We are artists.

Every tool is available — performance, painting, costume, incantation, improv, ceremony, gathering.

"The paintings are a good image of us. Full of life-force and movement. Sensual and energetic. Multiple beings in interaction with one another and with meaningful landscapes of mind and world. Always already in the middle of a journey."

The container we hold

We distribute responsibility and power as widely as possible. Everyone here is a potential leader — the one who initiates.

We care for each other with genuine loyalty and show up when it counts.

We create an atmosphere that is safe enough for vulnerability, with room for wildness to emerge as we drop in.

We are proactive

We make things happen. We don’t wait for permission. We are artists whose work is not a hobby — it is central to who we are and what we bring to the world.

This is the land of plenty. It isn’t a physical space.
It is a mindset.
